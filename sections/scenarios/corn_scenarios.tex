\subsubsection*{End-of-Century Projections}

The following pages present projected agricultural damages under various climate and socioeconomic scenarios. Each scenario combines:
\begin{itemize}
    \item \textbf{Year}: 2100 (end-of-century)
    \item \textbf{SSP}: Shared Socioeconomic Pathway (SSP2, SSP3, SSP4)
    \item \textbf{RCP}: Representative Concentration Pathway (RCP 4.5, RCP 8.5)
    \item \textbf{Variant}: High or Low damage estimate
\end{itemize}
